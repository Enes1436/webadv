The Pizza Italia Story

It all began in a small kitchen in Florence. With a handful of secret recipes and a heart full of passion, our founder, Giovanni Romano, dreamed of bringing authentic Italian food to the world. Decades later, Pizza Italia continues to share that love of food and family with every guest.
